All About Hack-a-bot

The criteria for the Participation in the Hackathon are..

  • The team can have maximum 4 members.
  • Everyone in the team should have finished any one of the RPA developer foundation course in Uipath Academy.
  • RPA developer foundation : Course
  • RPA Citizen Developer Foundation : Course

All the paricipants have to upload their badges in the time of registration to the Hackathon, only then they can participate in the Hackathon.
